FAQ
Facts - Answers - Questions

1. Is it legal/safe for restaurants to donate food?

Yes! This misunderstanding comes from years ago, when there were more hurdles to donation. However, since 1996, it has been legal, at the federal level, for restaurants to donate food thanks to the Bill Emerson Good Samaritan Food Doantion Act, signed under President Bill Clinton.

2. Why should I donate food?

Other than helping local families and the environment, restaurants can deduct their donations on their taxes.
